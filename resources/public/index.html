CLJS Mines

Description

A Minesweeper clone written in ClojureScript.

  • Left-click to sweep a blank cell (avoid the mines!)
  • Right-click a blank cell to plant a flag
  • If a numbered cell is surrounded by precisely the correct number of flags, left-click the cell to sweep its unflagged neighbours
  • You do not have to flag every mine; just clear every safe cell
